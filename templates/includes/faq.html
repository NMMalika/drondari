Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment with Dr. Ondari.

Does Dr. Ondari use SHA for surgical cases?

Yes. Dr. Ondari accepts eligible patients under the Social Health Authority (SHA) for approved surgical procedures.

Does Dr. Ondari see patients with SHA Mwalimu Comprehensive?

Yes. Patients covered under SHA Mwalimu Comprehensive are welcome.

Does Dr. Ondari see patients with SHA Usalama?

Yes. Active SHA Usalama members are attended to.

Where can I find Dr. Ondari?

Simply complete the Book Appointment form and our team will guide you to the appropriate clinic location.

Do you provide antenatal and postnatal care?

Yes. We provide complete pregnancy care from the first trimester through delivery and after childbirth.

What gynaecological conditions do you treat?

We manage fibroids, infertility, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, menstrual disorders, menopause, cervical screening, family planning, pelvic pain and many other women's health conditions.
